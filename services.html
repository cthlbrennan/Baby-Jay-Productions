Audio and Podcasting Services, Boutique and Bespoke

Whether you are representing your brand, your company or yourself as an independent podcaster, Baby Jay Productions has you covered.

Baby Jay Productions has the technical and creative experience and know-how to ensure that your project is defined, refined, deliverable and effective. By following an effective and meaningful step-by-step process from ideation and marketing to pre-production, production and post-production, our clients benefit from a bespoke partnership that will lead to the realisation of their project. Have a look at the services we provide below:

Concept Consultation

We help you bring your podcast vision to life. Our show development service includes brainstorming concepts, creating episode outlines, and developing a consistent format that resonates with your target audience. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refine your existing show, we provide the guidance and expertise to make your podcast compelling and successful.

Social Media and Marketing Strategy

Increase your podcast's visibility with our marketing and promotion services. We create tailored marketing strategies, including social media promotion, email campaigns, and collaborations with influencers in your niche. Our goal is to grow your listener base and build a strong, engaged community around your podcast.

Audio Editing

Our audio editing service ensures your podcast sounds professional and polished. We remove background noise, adjust volume levels, and eliminate any unwanted pauses or errors. We use industry-standard software to enhance the clarity and quality of your audio, making sure every episode is crisp, clear, and engaging for your listeners.

Monetisation Strategies

Turn your podcast into a profitable venture with our monetization strategies. We provide guidance on various revenue streams, such as sponsorships, affiliate marketing, and listener donations. We help you identify the best monetization options for your podcast and support you in implementing these strategies effectively.

Technical Consultation and Set-up

Our technical consultation and setup service offers expert advice on the best equipment and software for high-quality podcasts. We help you choose the right microphones, audio interfaces, and recording software within your budget. Additionally, we set up your recording environment for optimal sound quality and can provide ongoing technical support. Focus on creating without stressing about the technical details.

Podcast Hosting and Distribution

We take care of the technical side of getting your podcast online. Our hosting and distribution service ensures your episodes are uploaded to all major podcast platforms, including Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and Google Podcasts. We manage your RSS feed and provide detailed analytics so you can track your podcast's performance and reach.

Don't see what you're looking for above? If it's relating to audio, it's something we're likely to be able to handle, so drop us a line for a free consultation and we'll assist in any way we can.
