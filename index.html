About Baby Jay Productions

Siobhan Brosnan is a full-time professional in the broadcasting industry. She continues to produce high-quality shows and segments for Radio Kerry. She has written and produced award-winning documentaries and radio plays, and is highly experienced and proficient in all areas of radio and podcast production, from the technical to the commercial.

She is passionate about storytelling, crafting high-quality, engaging narratives that captivate your audience and leave a lasting impact. Her dedication ensures your stories are both compelling and memorable, resonating deeply with listeners.

Make Your Voice Stand Out

The Irish podcast industry boasts high listenership and offers unparalleled opportunities to reach and develop your audience. However, many potential podcasters face stumbling blocks such as lack of technical expertise, unclear identity, poor vision, and inadequate planning.

Baby Jay Productions bridges this gap by providing expert guidance on equipment and recording procedures, professional audio editing services, branding, deployment on streaming services, and online marketing.
